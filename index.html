Entrepreneurship Canada

Our world today is filled with conflicting frames that dictate how we think, work, dream and attempt to change things for the better; business versus charity, profit versus purpose, career versus calling.
A generation of women and youth is emerging that is not only questioning those frames, but also redrawing them, and unifying under new models of entrepreneurship.
On June 15th Canadian Entrepreneurship Commission will launch a bold new program to help shape Canada into global powerhouse for these new models of 21st century entrepreneurship.
